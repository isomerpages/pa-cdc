<p><strong><em>Claymaking workshop for seniors</em></strong>
</p>
<p>2025</p>
<p>Clay</p>
<p>20 × 15 cm</p>
<p>Collection of Caregiving Welfare Association</p>
<p>This collection of ceramic artworks was created by seniors during a clay
making workshop, and celebrates their lived experiences and hopes for Singapore’s
future. Each piece was crafted with care by the seniors and are inspired
by their personal memories.</p>
<p>These works honour the voices of older generations, bridging past and
present through forms shaped by hand and heart, celebrating the vital role
seniors play in shaping Singapore’s journey.</p>
<p><u>Community Artists (Seniors):</u>
</p>
<p>Chiang Yit Beng, Chua Kooi Teng, Lee Poh Kim, Chris Ong Lay Khim, Puaw
Sey Geak, Quek Bee Ng, Sim Lily, Tan Geok Tee Judy, Tee Ah Lap, Yeo Chor
Hoon Doreen</p>
