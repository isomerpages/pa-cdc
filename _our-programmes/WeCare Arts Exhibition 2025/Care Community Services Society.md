<p><strong>Project Description:</strong>
</p>
<p>This intergenerational art project brings together children, youth and
seniors to create a shared visual tapestry of Singapore’s journey. Each
small canvas captures a personal memory, reflecting the past and dreams
for the future. Together, the 30 canvases form a collective portrait of
our evolving community — celebrating diversity, connection and the enduring
spirit of SG60 and beyond.</p>
