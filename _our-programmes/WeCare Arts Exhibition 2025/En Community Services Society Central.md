<p><strong>Title: </strong>生生不息, 巳巳如意 (Everlasting Prosperity and All Wishes
Come True)</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Central___En_Community_Services_Society__2_.jpg">
</div>
<p><strong>Project Description:</strong>
</p>
<p>Celebrating Singapore's 60th birthday, a year of the Snake (巳)! Our seniors
at En Community Social Services extend warm wishes for enduring prosperity
and happiness (生生不息，巳巳如意).</p>
<p>Join us in celebrating with a unique art display: two frames featuring
nine beautifully engraved ceramic tiles each, and around 100 more tiles
welcoming visitors at the entrance. Each tile carries a special blessing
for SG60.</p>
<p><strong>Artist(s): </strong>Vanessa Tong</p>
